Hi, I’m Mario Del Mastro

Dr. Mario Del Mastro: Specialized in Experimental Microfluidics and Transport Phenomena

Dr. Mario Del Mastro holds a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering from the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology. His expertise lies in microfluidics, porous media, and interfacial momentum transfer, combining experimental micro-PIV, asymptotic modeling, and advanced microfabrication. He has authored peer-reviewed publications in Physics of Fluids and received the Jacobs Prize for Excellent Publication.
